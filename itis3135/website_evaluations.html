CRAP Website Evalutaions

Good Site Design: Ocean Health Index

This site is a source to gain knowledge abou the ocean's health and how to improve it. It is very well designed and uses CRAP principles well.

  • C: There is a good sense of contrast in this site, with white text being on dark blue backgrounds, and vice versa in other cases.
  • R: The website stays consistent with fonts and stye, using slightly rounded boxes for most of its content and maintaining use of blue gradients.
  • A: The alignment on the header is spaced well, and the main content has great even alignment for its content, separating into halves or thirds depending on what is necessary.
  • P: Every Element has just enough space so that it does not feel too isolated or too cramped, and the whitespace is used to make the website look cleaner.

Bad Site Design: Arngren

This is a Finnish classifieds site that people use to buy and sell technology and other items. This sit does not use CRAP principles well at all.

  • C: There is not much contrast used in this site at all, as all of the sections have the same background and border.
  • R: This website is very inconsistent. Many objects have different border sizes, if they have a border at all, and they all have different box sizes.
  • A: There is no clear sense of alignment on this site. Everything is just put on the page without any rhyme or reason.
  • P: Everything is WAY too cramped on this website.
