Sushi and Community.

For my design agency 1893 Brand Studio, I was tasked with social media design for Spicy Nine, a Chapel Hill-based sushi restaurant that implements a modern ambience with good food and fun vibes.


With a mixture of bright Instagram posts focusing on food as the centerpiece, and a strong highlighting of venue and deals, the campaign sought to bring an overall larger sense of community behind Spicy Nine, creating a +1,015% increase in follower engagement and increasing follower growth by 108% in 4 months.
