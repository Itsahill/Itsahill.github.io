Role: Art Director, Copywriter, and Strategist
Insight: Healthcare in America is a complex, expensive system with a lot of technical lingo and know-how. The average American still reads at an eight-grade reading level.

Solution: A public health education and awareness campaign centered on a quick, reliable booklet available for free in health centers and a website with carefully integrated AI tools to help patients navigate their health journeys.

Booklet

The booklet is sectioned into digestible content, with sections for thought process, medical terminology, and information on interactions between stakeholders in US healthcare systems written in plain language.

Website

The website contains a compressive A-Z lexicon of the diagnoses in the International Classification of Diseases (ICD-10-CM), the most common system used for medical recordkeeping and billing. Searching simple plain language terms like "stomachache" and "head hurts" will be translated into medical terms that pulls up a reference of diagnoses that can tie to the terms mentioned.


The LLM Chatbot summary writer helps you understand the care landscape around a condition. When you enter a concern or a diagnosis, it cross-references verified clinician-written resources and structured codes from the ICD-10-CM to explain what your condition typically involves, what questions patients often ask, and what care decisions may come next. The result is a clear, plain-language summary you can review on your own, or share with your primary care provider to guide a more informed conversation.
